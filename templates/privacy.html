Privacy Policy
Arrow Industries OAuth Verification System

1. Information We Collect

In compliance with 15 U.S.C. § 6502 (COPPA) and GDPR Article 13, we collect the following personal information:

  • Discord Data: User ID, username, discriminator, global name, and avatar URL
  • ROBLOX Data: User ID, username, display name, and avatar headshot URL
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser information, and session data
  • Verification Data: Timestamps, verification status, and linking history

2. How We Use Your Information

Under GDPR Article 6(1)(b) and 15 U.S.C. § 6501(8), we process your data for:

  • Account verification and authentication
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized access
  • Maintaining service security and integrity
  • Compliance with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be disclosed only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations under 18 U.S.C. § 2703
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • In case of business transfer or merger

4. Data Security

In accordance with GDPR Article 32 and NIST Cybersecurity Framework, we implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection practices

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR Articles 15-22 and CCPA § 1798.100-130, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law under 44 U.S.C. § 3101 (Federal Records Act).

7. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred internationally in compliance with GDPR Chapter V and adequacy decisions. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for all transfers.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Under ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC, we obtain consent for non-essential cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

In compliance with 15 U.S.C. § 6502 (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on our service, with 30 days advance notice as required by applicable law.

11. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer through our official Discord server or support channels.

12. Supervisory Authority

EU residents have the right to lodge complaints with their local supervisory authority. US residents may contact the Federal Trade Commission at ftc.gov.
