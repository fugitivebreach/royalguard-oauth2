Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Royal Guard OAuth2 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Roblox verification service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our verification service, we collect the following information:

Discord Information

  • Discord username
  • Discord user ID

Roblox Information

  • Roblox username
  • Roblox user ID
  • Roblox profile URL

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Geolocation data (country, region, coordinates)
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Verification timestamp

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Verification: To link your Roblox account with your Discord profile
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent verification attempts
  • Server Management: To help server administrators manage their communities
  • Geolocation Tracking: To identify suspicious activity and enforce server rules

4. Data Storage and Retention

Important: Verification sessions are temporary and expire after 2 minutes. Completed verification data is stored in-memory and may be cleared when the service restarts. For production deployments, data may be stored in a database for server administration purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your verification data is shared only with:

  • The Discord server administrators where you are verifying
  • Roblox, through their OAuth2 service (as required for authentication)

6. Third-Party Services

Our service uses the following third-party services:

  • Roblox OAuth2: For Roblox account authentication
  • IP-API.com: For geolocation data based on IP addresses

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Secure OAuth2 authentication flow
  • Time-limited verification sessions
  • One-time use verification links

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about the data we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your verification data
  • Opt-out of the verification process at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users who meet Discord's and Roblox's minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the Royal Guard server administrators.
